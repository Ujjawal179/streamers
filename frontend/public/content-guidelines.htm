Content Guidelines for Streamers.media

Effective Date: October 24, 2024

Streamers.media is a platform that enables creators to sell slots for branded content on their live channels. To maintain a safe and positive environment for all users, we have established these Content Guidelines. All creators and brands are expected to adhere to these guidelines when creating and posting content on the platform.

1. Prohibited Content

The following categories of content are strictly prohibited on Streamers.media:

  • Illegal Activity: Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities, including but not limited to drug trafficking, human trafficking, illegal gambling, and terrorism.
  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes violence against, threatens, or attacks others based on race, ethnicity, national origin, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, religious affiliation, age, disability, or disease.  
  • Harassment and Bullying: Content that harasses, intimidates, or bullies an individual or group.
  • Sexually Explicit Content: Content that is pornographic or sexually suggestive, including nudity, depictions of sexual acts, and sexually explicit language.
  • Exploitation of Minors: Content that exploits, abuses, or endangers children.
  • Violence and Graphic Content: Content that depicts graphic violence, gore, or animal cruelty.
  • Harmful and Dangerous Content: Content that promotes self-harm, suicide, eating disorders, or other dangerous behavior.
  • Misinformation and Disinformation: Content that spreads false or misleading information with the intent to deceive or harm.
  • Privacy Violations: Content that violates the privacy of others, including sharing personal information without consent.
  • Impersonation: Content that impersonates another person or entity.
  • Spam and Misleading Content: Content that is irrelevant, repetitive, or misleading, including clickbait and false advertising.
  • Intellectual Property Infringement: Content that infringes on the copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights of others.

2. Restricted Content

The following categories of content are restricted and require careful consideration:

  • Weapons: Content that depicts or promotes the use of weapons, including firearms, explosives, and other dangerous objects.
  • Alcohol and Tobacco: Content that depicts or promotes the use of alcohol or tobacco products.
  • Drugs and Controlled Substances: Content that depicts or promotes the use of illegal drugs or controlled substances.
  • Gambling: Content that promotes or facilitates gambling.
  • Political Content: Content that is political in nature, including endorsements of political candidates or parties.

3. Content Moderation

Streamers.media uses a combination of automated and human moderation to enforce these Content Guidelines. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines or that we deem objectionable for any reason.

4. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content that you believe violates these Content Guidelines, please report it to us immediately. You can report content by using the reporting tools provided on the platform or by contacting us directly.

5. Consequences of Violations

Creators who repeatedly violate these Content Guidelines may face consequences, including but not limited to:

  • Removal of content
  • Suspension of account
  • Termination of account

6. Disclaimer

These Content Guidelines are subject to change at any time. We encourage you to review them regularly.
